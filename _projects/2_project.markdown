My team and I designed a experiment as a part of undergraduate lecture PSY3012. 
We hypothesized that people would eat more within a low stressful environment while eat less within a high stressful environment. The experimental design was 3X2 (i.e., High stress group, Low stress group, Control group X Pre, Post intervention). 

Before the intervention, we measured each subject's stress by stress calculating app. In addition, conducted a survey with their stress level. And after the intervention, we measured stress with the same app and told subject to take chocolate as much as they want to eat. Also, we conducted a survey with their stress level about the test stress. 

The intervention which make up the stress for each group was take a difficult test. For the high stress group is monitored by a supervisor and told the group a short mean finish time of the test by others. And plant one of the researcher to end the test much more faster than the high stress group. For the low stress group is told a group long finish time of the test by others and the researcher within the experiment end the test much more faster than the low stress group. For the control group they are told long ending time of test by others and researcher within the experiment don't end the test. 

We conducted the pilot experiment with N=5.
